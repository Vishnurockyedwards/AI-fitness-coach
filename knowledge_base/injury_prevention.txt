Injury prevention: safer training principles

Warm-up
- 5–10 minutes of light cardio + joint mobility.
- Do a few lighter "ramp-up" sets before heavy working sets.

Progression
- Increase weight/reps gradually (avoid big week-to-week jumps).
- Keep 1–3 reps in reserve on most sets; push close to failure selectively.

Technique
- Use controlled reps and full range of motion you can control.
- Stop a set if form breaks down significantly.

Recovery
- Sleep matters: consistent sleep supports performance and recovery.
- Plan rest days and deload weeks if fatigue accumulates.

Pain vs. effort
- Muscle burn and fatigue are normal.
- Sharp pain, joint pain, numbness, or persistent pain are warning signs—stop and reassess.
